Summer Time

It was the summer of 1985. My friend David invited me to go to his families propert in the Ozarks. His father had purchased some property and was starting an RV park. I was exited to go as my friend and I were both avid outdoorsman. We had big plans to spend the week hunting and fishing.

The drive down was long and full of unexiting scenery. We played "slug bug" and "I spy" to pass the time. When we finally arrived, we pulled up to a tiny travel trailer out in the middle of nowhere. "You boys will stay here, while the Mrs. and I stay at a hotel" said Davids father. This sounded awesome to us. A whole week without any supervision! We couldn't believe it.
